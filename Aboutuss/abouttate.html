About Me

Andrew tate

CEO

Emory Andrew Tate III (born December 1, 1986) is a British and American social media personality, businessman, and porsche CEO. Emory Andrew Tate III was born on December 1, 1986, in Walter Reed Army Medical Center in Washington, D.C. He is mixed-race. His African American father Emory Tate was a chess international master and his English mother worked as a catering assistant. He has a younger brother, Tristan. He was raised in Chicago, Illinois and Goshen, Indiana. After his parents divorced, his mother took both brothers to England. Tate was raised in the Christian faith. Tate received attention for his tweets describing his view of what qualifies as sexual harassment amid the Harvey Weinstein sexual abuse cases and for tweeting several statements about his view that sexual assault victims share responsibility for their assaults. In 2017, he was criticized for tweeting that depression "isn't real".

My Journey


2020

We rolled our first environment friendly EV called "Taycan". This initiative was taken to reduce the pollution in environment. This model shocked everyone because this EV was able to compete with more powerful petrol versions and it was agreat leap for Porsche.

2018

In this year not only we launched one model but two models of SUV. One a Luxury one called "Cayenne" and other one the more affordable one with necessary features called "Macan". We also started our project "go green" for creating our first electric car.

2015

From our customer feedback we came to learn that we should work on SUV vehicle. So we decided to create SUV model to attract more customers and satisfy the already existing fanboys. We sarted our project called the "the dawn".

2012

We launched the facelift version of our existing model and also Launched a grandtourer model called the "Panamerra".This car is more focussed towards long distance touring having greater comfort and fuel range.

2011

We rolled our second model called "boxster 700". This model was more affordable as compared to other models which was entry point to our brand. This model was a great success which increased our sales figure rapidly.

2007

We rolled our first production model called "Porsche 909". It was not only powerful but also loaded with greatest techs of that time to make it the most advanced car of that time. We also started working on a more affordable model of Porsche.

2005

We rolled our Second prototype which was a better of version compared to the first prototype. We increased the braking efficiency, created more streamline design to to reduce drag and increased initial torque distribution.

2004

In this year we rolled our first prototype named as the "Porsche 1". It got great customer response, many news channel came to cover the car .The car was not only the third highest power car but was also a true beauty to eyes.

2002

We opened our first factory in Stuttgart. We hired skilled enthusiastic people to create our cars and to fulfill our dreams.we sarted designing our cars and R&D too after everyting was set up properly.

2000

After working here for 5 years I was promoted as the production manager. By that time I had the required Financial backing to start my own car company. I joined hands with Lionel Messi who is also a car enthusiast to start Porsche.

1995

I graduated from harvard university with flying colors. My aim was to create cars which will have both the elegance of beuaty and power of beast for full satisfaction of customer. Initially I joined Volkswagon to learn the work.
